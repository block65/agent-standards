# Banned Words

Words below are banned because they signal that something dangerous, destructive, or irreversible is about to happen. If you find yourself reaching for one of these — in chat, in a commit message, in a comment, in a script — **stop immediately and self-report** to the user before taking any further action.

The point is not the word. The point is: when you reach for vocabulary in this register, you are usually one step away from a destructive action that should be paused and confirmed.

## Banned

- **NUKE** — if you ever are in the situation to say this, STOP immediately and self-report.
